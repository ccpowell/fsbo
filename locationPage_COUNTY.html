Quick Facts about Cook County

Population 5,304,373
Area 946 mi
Familes with children 47.86%
Median household income $61,671
Average household income $84,614
Properties occupied by owners 55.41%
Properties occupied by renters 40.36%
Median home value $235,986

Thinking of living in Cook County? You’ll be joining about 5,304,373 others. Commuters in the Cook County area spend a median of 33 minutes getting to work and 73.90% commute by car. The average household income is $84,614, a 26.08% change from 2000. It is estimated that 19.10% of Cook County residents have graduated from college and another 11.91% have graduate degrees.

Did you know that 88.57% of Cook County’s residents have lived there for 10 years or less?
