Living in Illinois

Did you know that 89.25% of the 12,951,671 residents of Illinois have re-located to the state within the past decade? By making the move to Illinois, you’ll find yourself in good company.

The average household income in Illinois is $80,286. This is a 23.40% change from the average income in the year 2000. 18.66% of Illinois residents have a college degree, with 10.62% of residents in possession of a graduate degree.

And while the housing market has had its ups and downs over the past several years nationwide, the median home price in Illinois is $192,867.
