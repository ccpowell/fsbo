Quick Facts about Chicago

Thinking of living in Chicago? You’ll be joining about 2,887,897 others. Commuters in the Chicago area spend a median of 35 minutes getting to work and 63.23% commute by car. The average household income is $73,175, a 25.78% change from 2000. It is estimated that 17.68% of Chicago residents have graduated from college and another 11.39% have graduate degrees.

Did you know that 89.26% of Chicago’s residents have lived there for 10 years or less?
